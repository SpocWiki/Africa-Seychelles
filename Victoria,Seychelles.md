# [[Victoria,Seychelles]] 

#is_/same_as :: [[../../../../../WikiData/WD~Victoria,3940|WD~Victoria,3940]] 

## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> Victoria is the capital and largest city of Seychelles, 
> situated on the north-eastern side of Mahé, the archipelago's main island. 
> 
> The city was first established as the seat of the British colonial government. 
> In 2010, the population of Greater Victoria (including the suburbs) was 26,450 (26.66%) 
> out of the country's total population of 99,202.
>
> The port is known as Port Victoria.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Victoria,%20Seychelles)
