
## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> **Praslin** (French pronunciation: [pʁalɛ̃]) is the second largest island (38.5 km2) of the Inner Seychelles, 
> lying 44 km (27 mi) northeast of Mahé. 
> 
> Praslin has a population of around 7,533 people and comprises two administrative districts: 
> - Baie Sainte Anne and 
> - Grand' Anse. 
> 
> The main settlements are the Baie Ste Anne, Anse Volbert and Grand' Anse.
>
